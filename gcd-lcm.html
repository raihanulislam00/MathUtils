GCD & LCM Relationship

The product of the GCD and LCM of two numbers equals the product of the numbers themselves.

GCD(a,b) × LCM(a,b) = a × b
🧮

Euclidean Algorithm

The Euclidean algorithm is an efficient way to compute the GCD of two numbers, dating back to ancient Greece.

Over 2,300 years old

LCM in Real Life

LCM is used to find common time intervals, such as when two events will coincide again.
