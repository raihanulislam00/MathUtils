📋 Quick Reference

Control Characters (0-31)

Special non-printable characters used for text formatting and control

  • 0: NULL
  • 9: TAB
  • 10: LF (Line Feed)
  • 13: CR (Carriage Return)
  • 27: ESC (Escape)

Printable Characters (32-126)

Visible characters including letters, numbers, and symbols

  • 32: Space
  • 48-57: Digits (0-9)
  • 65-90: Uppercase (A-Z)
  • 97-122: Lowercase (a-z)

Extended ASCII (128-255)

Extended characters for international and special symbols

  • 128-159: Control characters
  • 160-255: Extended symbols
  • Used for accented characters
  • Mathematical symbols
